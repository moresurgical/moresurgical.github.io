MoreSurgical is India’s first concept store focused exclusively for rehabilitation, mobility, and home healthcare products and services. We aim to provide the customer a complete experience about solutions to health challenges and thereby educating them about various ways to solve their problems. We work with various international brands and NGOs to provide the best class service for those who are challenged. Our portfolio include: mobility solutions, home healthcare, pediatric and special needs children care products, elderly and assisted daily living products, mother and baby care products, diabetics and orthopedic solutions, hospital and surgical supplies, and respiratory care.
